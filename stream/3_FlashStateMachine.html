State machines in Flash

Flash's movieclips were sort of state machines. In their most basic form, they transition from one state (keyframe) to the next on each new frame. However, a movieclip's keyframes could also be controlled more explicitly. Each keyframe could be assigned a label, and a Flash developer could programmatically cause the transition from one keyframe to another, via the gotoAndPlay or gotoAndStop functions.

I remember often using movieclips as state machines. I wonder if that contributed to the seeming ease of making games in Flash.
